Welcome to

University Institute of Technology, RGPV

UIT-RGPV, Bhopal is a community of teachers, staff & students, who are dedicated to the achievement of a shared vision of excellence. The community believes in renewing and transferring knowledge & skills and the development of positive & purposeful attitudes and values.

The Institution is situated in Bhopal, a city well known for its natural beauty & lakes. The city is well connected to the other parts of the country by air, rail, and road.
